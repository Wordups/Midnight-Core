## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the acceptable use of Takeoff LLC information systems, including the Midnight platform, AWS infrastructure, source code repositories, and SaaS tools.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors, and authorized agents accessing Takeoff systems.

## 3. Definitions

- **Takeoff Systems:** Any computing resource, application, or data store owned, leased, or operated by Takeoff LLC
- **Personal Use:** Activity unrelated to Takeoff business operations
- **Prohibited Activity:** Any use that violates law, regulation, or Takeoff policy

## 4. Policy Statements

### 4.1 Authorized Use

Takeoff systems are provided for the conduct of Takeoff business. Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with business operations, consume significant resources, or violate this policy.

### 4.2 Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly prohibited:
- Unauthorized access to systems, data, or accounts
- Sharing credentials with any party, including other Takeoff personnel
- Installing unauthorized software on Takeoff-managed devices
- Storing customer data on personal devices or unauthorized cloud services
- Using Takeoff systems for illegal activity, harassment, or content prohibited by applicable law
- Bypassing security controls, including MFA, VPN, or DLP mechanisms
- Disclosing customer-uploaded documents or generated artifacts to any external party

### 4.3 Confidentiality

All customer data accessed through Takeoff systems is confidential. Personnel are prohibited from copying, transmitting, or retaining customer data outside of authorized business workflows.

### 4.4 Monitoring

Takeoff reserves the right to monitor all activity on Takeoff systems. Users have no expectation of privacy on Takeoff-owned or operated systems.

### 4.5 Personal Devices

Use of personal devices to access Takeoff systems requires enrollment in Takeoff's mobile device management (MDM) or equivalent endpoint controls. Personal devices that cannot meet baseline security requirements may not access production systems.

## 5. Roles and Responsibilities

- **Founder:** Approves exceptions, enforces policy
- **All Personnel:** Read, acknowledge, and comply with this policy annually

## 6. Enforcement

Violations may result in access revocation, disciplinary action up to and including termination, and legal action where applicable.

## 7. Review Cadence

Reviewed annually.
